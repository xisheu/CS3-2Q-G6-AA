About Us

Our Vision

To co-create an improved, appropriate, and proper learning environment for all students nationwide. Rather than suffering in these school infrastructures, we will find a way for students to benefit from them. To create and provide fair treatment to all students, regardless of class level, race, age, etc.

Our Mission

We aim to address and create possible solutions regarding the social issue of educational inequality that students tend to experience on a day-to-day basis. We want to avoid these occurrences for students to not suffer, and instead, be in a more healthy environment.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Service, Fairness, and Commitment.
